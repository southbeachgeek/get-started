Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the live web development session.

Session Details

What is included in the 1-hour live web development session?

The session includes one hour of real-time WordPress website development conducted over Zoom. A full website foundation is built during this time. All technical work is handled live while the client watches, gives feedback, or simply observes. By the end of the hour, a professional and functional WordPress website is live and ready to use.

What platform is used to build the website?

The website is built using WordPress. A fresh installation is completed during the session, and all customization and setup are done within that platform.

How does the session work in terms of participation?

Clients can participate actively by providing input and asking questions during the session. Alternatively, they may choose to observe while the website is built according to their initial direction.

Technical Details

Is web hosting setup part of the session?

Yes. Web hosting setup is included. The hosting account is configured, and a domain is connected to make the website live.

Domain registration (around $15/year) and hosting are separate costs paid to the chosen provider.

A recommended hosting option at $35/month offers high speed and reliability, allowing for a more productive use of the hour. However, clients may choose any hosting provider they prefer.

Can a domain name be connected during the session?

Yes. A purchased domain name is connected to the hosting account to ensure the website goes live during the session.

Are premium tools or plugins included?

Yes. The session includes a lifetime license to Astra Pro and Spectra Pro, valued at over $600. These tools allow for enhanced design customization and include features such as:

  • A responsive, mobile-optimized layout
  • Integration of brand colors and fonts
  • Setup of Home, About, and Contact pages with starter content
  • Logo integration
  • Contact form setup
  • Basic SEO and security plugin installation
  • eCommerce-ready functionality
Is help provided for setting up a domain-based email address?

Yes. Assistance is included to help create a professional email address linked to the domain through the hosting provider.

Content & Results

What content is required before the session?

No content is required beforehand. Professional starter content and images can be provided during the session. However, any business information, goals, or branding assets shared in advance will enhance the customization of the website.

What is the expected outcome by the end of the session?

By the end of the session, the client will have a live, functional, and professional WordPress website that is easy to manage and ready for visitors.

Can additional features or content be added later?

Yes. While the initial hour covers the full site foundation, more advanced features, additional content, or extended product catalogs can be added through future 1-hour sessions.

Will the client receive full access to the website?

Yes. All login credentials are provided to the client, who retains full ownership of the website. Editing content, images, and layout is made simple with a user-friendly backend, allowing for easy future updates.

Booking & Support

What if more time is needed beyond the 1-hour session?

If additional development is required, more sessions can be scheduled. Each additional session is one hour in length and billed at $150 per hour.

Is ongoing support available after the session?

Yes. Lifetime support is provided via Fiverr messages. Clients can reach out for assistance, clarification, or general guidance regarding their website at any time.

Is it possible to receive a recording of the Zoom session?

Yes. The Zoom session can be recorded upon request and provided as a personal video tutorial for future reference.
